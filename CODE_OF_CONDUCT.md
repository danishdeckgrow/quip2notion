# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

**Acceptable behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to **danish@deckgrow.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
